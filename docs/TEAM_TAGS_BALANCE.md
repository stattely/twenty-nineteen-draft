# Team Tags & Balance V2.6

## Rating cap

- 99: SSW 2014
- 98: SKT 2015
- 97: T1 2023
- All other teams are capped at 96 at team level.

## Team tags

Good tags represent identity already baked into the team rating.
They are shown for readability, but they do not add free power just because the team exists.

During a run, extra power comes from:
- player/player synergies
- region/country/team/org affinity
- Meta
- Quest
- Chrono powers

## Tag colors

- +5 Gold: legendary
- +4 Yellow: elite
- +3 Purple: very strong
- +2 Blue: solid
- +1 Green: light positive
- 0 Gray: neutral identity
- -1 Red: flaw

Bad tags are capped at -3 per team.
