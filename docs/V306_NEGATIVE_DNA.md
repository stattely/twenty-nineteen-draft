# V3.0.6 Negative DNA

Adds anti-synergy / risk traits:
- Flipper Mecánico: too many hands/carry/creative.
- Triple Carry Tax: too many carries competing for resources.
- Sin Shotcaller: no clear macro voice.
- Sin Botón De Inicio: damage without engage.
- Vision Gap: no vision/control/shotcall.
- Early Game En Deuda: too much scaling without tempo.
- Overforce Chain: too much creative skirmish without stability.
- Dos Lados Piden Recursos: top carry and bot carry with no shotcaller.
- SoloQ Comms: too many unrelated org/region profiles.
- Hopium Tax: full West vs endbosses.
- Too Many Chiefs: too many shotcallers.
- No Weakside: everyone wants resources.

Risks subtract from synergy, show as red chips, and add controlled volatility in map simulation.
